What we do

Industrial electrical expertise, end to end.

From precision rewinding to wholesale electrical supplies — we keep Fiji's factories, hotels, utilities, and telecom networks running.

01

Motor & Generator Rewinding

AC and DC motor rewinding for pumps, compressors, fans, and industrial machinery. Complete generator and alternator overhauls with Class F & H insulation, megger testing, and performance certification.

02

Electrical Supplies

Wholesale and retail electrical components — cables, switchgear, motors, bearings, and industrial equipment. Trusted supplier to sugar mills, hotels, water treatment plants, and manufacturing facilities.

03

Pump & Equipment Repair

Submersible, centrifugal, and borehole pump repairs. On-site service across Viti Levu with mobile workshop teams for emergency removal, installation, and diagnostics.

04

Testing & Preventive Maintenance

Winding resistance checks, vibration analysis, and scheduled maintenance programs to extend equipment life and prevent costly downtime.

About us

Fiji's trusted winding house.

For over two decades, Lal's Motor Winders has been the go-to specialist when machinery stops. Our Suva workshop houses modern winding machines, precision testing equipment, and a team of certified technicians who treat every job as critical infrastructure.

We understand that downtime costs money. That's why we offer emergency 48-hour service, transparent quoting, pickup and delivery across Viti Levu, and a warranty on every rewind. From sugar mills to telecom towers — when Fiji needs power restored, they call Lal's.
