Our Services

Comprehensive electrical and mechanical solutions for industrial, commercial, and residential clients across Fiji.

Motor Rewinding

Complete AC and DC motor rewinding for pumps, fans, compressors, and industrial machinery. Precision winding with quality insulation materials.

Pump Repair

Submersible, centrifugal, and borehole pump repairs. Impeller replacement, seal repairs, and performance testing to restore full capacity.

Generator Services

Alternator rewinding, AVR replacement, and full generator overhauls. Keep your backup power systems running when you need them most.

Testing & Diagnostics

Megger testing, winding resistance checks, and vibration analysis. Comprehensive reports before and after every repair.

On-Site Service

Mobile workshop teams available for on-site motor removal, installation, and emergency repairs at your facility across Fiji.

Preventive Maintenance

Scheduled maintenance programs to extend equipment life, reduce downtime, and prevent costly breakdowns for your operations.
