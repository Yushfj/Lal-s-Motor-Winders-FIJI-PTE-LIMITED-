What we do

Industrial electrical expertise, end to end.

From precision rewinding to wholesale electrical supplies — we keep Fiji's factories, hotels, utilities, and telecom networks running.

01

Motor & Generator Rewinding

AC and DC motor rewinding for pumps, compressors, fans, and industrial machinery. Complete generator overhauls with Class F & H insulation and megger certification.

02

Electrical Supplies

Wholesale cables, switchgear, motors, bearings, and industrial equipment. Trusted by sugar mills, hotels, and manufacturing facilities across Fiji.

03

Pump & Equipment Repair

Submersible, centrifugal, and borehole pump repairs. On-site mobile workshop teams across Viti Levu for emergency removal and installation.

04

Testing & Maintenance

Winding resistance checks, vibration analysis, and scheduled maintenance to extend equipment life and prevent costly downtime.

About us

Fiji's trusted winding house.

For over two decades, Lal's Motor Winders has been the go-to specialist when machinery stops. Our Suva workshop houses modern winding machines, precision testing equipment, and certified technicians.

We offer emergency 48-hour service, transparent quoting, pickup and delivery across Viti Levu, and a warranty on every rewind. From sugar mills to telecom towers — when Fiji needs power restored, they call Lal's.
