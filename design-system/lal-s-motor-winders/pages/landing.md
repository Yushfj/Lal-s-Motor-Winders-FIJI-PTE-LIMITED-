# Landing Page Overrides

> Overrides `MASTER.md` for the marketing homepage only.

**Pattern:** Trust & Authority + Conversion (not Vibrant & Block-based)

**Section order:**
1. Hero (brand + credibility mission)
2. Services overview
3. Experience / trust signals
4. How we work
5. Contact / Get a quote

**Style direction:** Industrial professional — slate surfaces, stock-green CTA only, restrained motion (scroll reveal + hover). Avoid playful block layouts, neon accents, and purple AI gradients.

**Hero rules:**
- Full-bleed workshop imagery
- Brand name is the dominant first-viewport signal
- One headline, one supporting sentence, one CTA group
- No cards, badges, or floating overlays on hero media

**Typography note:** Keep Poppins + Open Sans from Master; increase display weight for brand lockup.
